Professional Development Planning

Introduction‚Äã

You may know, either from previous lessons or from your own research, that one of the best ways a recent graduate can improve their job search success is to take the time to differentiate. Learning additional skills allows you to be a good candidate for roles you might not be otherwise. It also will set you apart from other applicants who may be applying for the same job. Because this class is intended to give you freedom to explore, somewhat like a thesis project, there is no dedicated technical curriculum. It‚Äôs very important not to go in blind, however, so before you begin the Capstone class, we‚Äôre going to take some time to walk through the process organizing what your next few weeks will look like.

We‚Äôre going to do this by teaching you how to make a professional development plan. In this context, it‚Äôs going to guide your classroom learning, but this is also a valuable skill for longer term career success.
